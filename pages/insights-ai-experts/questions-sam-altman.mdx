# Questions for Sam Altman

In the interview, Chris Dossman reflects on what he would ask Sam Altman, the CEO of OpenAI, about the future of AI and its potential impacts. As a prominent figure in the AI industry, Altman's insights and perspectives are highly valued by researchers, developers, and entrepreneurs alike.

## Key Questions for Sam Altman

Chris might consider asking Sam Altman the following questions:

- **What are the most significant challenges in developing safe and beneficial AI systems?** Understanding the obstacles and potential pitfalls in AI development is crucial for creating robust and ethical AI solutions.

- **How can we ensure that AI systems align with human values and goals?** Ensuring that AI systems act in accordance with human values is a critical concern as AI becomes more advanced and influential in our lives.

- **What role do you see AI playing in shaping the future of work?** As discussed in the [AI and the Future of Work](/ai-future-work) section, AI has the potential to automate tedious tasks and transform various industries. Altman's perspective on this topic would be invaluable.

- **How can we foster collaboration between AI researchers, developers, and policymakers to address the societal implications of AI?** Developing AI responsibly requires input and cooperation from various stakeholders, and Altman's experience in navigating these relationships could provide valuable insights.
